About this area

Venice

Located in Venice's Castello neighbourhood, Ca' Bragadin Carabba is in the city centre and near a train station. The area's natural beauty can be seen at Grand Canal and Giardini della Biennale, while La Fenice Opera House and Palazzo Grassi are cultural highlights. Our guests love the property's quiet location.

About this property

Ca' Bragadin Carabba

This apartment building features express check-in, express check-out and tour/ticket assistance. WiFi in public areas is free. Additionally, multilingual staff, a garden and a front desk safe are on-site. Each apartment offers comforts like a memory foam bed and Egyptian cotton sheets, plus complimentary wireless Internet access and an LCD TV with satellite channels. Free wired Internet, a minibar and a hairdryer are among the other amenities available to guests. Housekeeping is available on request.
